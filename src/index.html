How Uplift Works

1

Gather

One person starts a session and shares the code. Everyone joins using the same code. You need at least 2 people to begin.

2

Write

Everyone writes a note of appreciation for each person in the session. Be specific and heartfelt‚Äîthe more personal, the more meaningful.

3

Share

Take turns picking notes and reading them aloud to the group. Since notes don't have author names attached, Uplift emphasizes the group supporting one another instead of individual relationships.

4

Reflect

At the end, you'll see all the notes written for you. Take a moment to appreciate the recognition from your team. You can copy or print the affirmations to read again later.

Tip: Uplift works best when everyone is present and engaged. Find a quiet moment when your group can focus together.
